More than 400 American bridges are failing.

An analysis of the 2022 National Bridge Inventory shows 410 bridges are structurally failing. The analysis includes all bridges that have been classified by the inventory as imminently failing in the deck, substructure and superstructure. The substructure includes bridge elements below the bridge's deck, including piers and abutments. The superstructure includes elements above the deck.
